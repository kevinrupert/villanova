The Bigger Picture

PHOTO: ANTHONY BUSA

Anthony Busa ’19 COE, landed dream opportunities to work with National Geographic and an independent photographer in 2017 and 2016, which took him deep into remote Mongolian and Tibetan communities.

Photographs he took on those excursions, displayed in the Villanova Art Gallery last fall, capture unique moments, as well as the spirit of cultures that are vanishing due to modernization and globalization. “As a photographer, I do my best to understand and appreciate different ways of life, and, through the photographs, I try to help others attain a meaningful understanding and appreciation,” Anthony says.
