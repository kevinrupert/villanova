Commencement 2018

Cheers and tears abounded as Villanova recognized and bid farewell to the Class of 2018. Faculty, staff, administrators, families and friends gathered on campus May 18 and 19 for the University’s 175th Commencement Ceremony and related celebrations. More than 3,180 bachelor’s, master’s, doctoral and professional degrees were conferred across six colleges.
